The home

This house is 1200 square ft with ocean and beach views.  Sleeps 8-10.  Has 3 Bedrooms, 2 Bathrooms. Amenities include: air conditioning, fireplace, washer, dryer, iron and ironing board, linens and towels provided, coffee maker, dishes and cooking utensils, dishwasher, toaster oven, blender, internet and cable, and flat screen TVs.  Outdoor Amenities include: private pool, fish cleaning sink, outdoor shower, charcoal grill, parking

This great property has all the amenities you need at a great price. The large screened in porch is perfect for relaxation. This family home has room for you, the kids and maybe the grandparents. One of the top beaches in the country is only a few steps away. This clean white sand beach is great for shelling, sand castles, and playing in the water. Fishing in the surf will yield you dinner. Relax by the pool at the end of a great day at the beach.  The island bike and walking path right in front of house and runs the length of the island. You will see many families and their dogs enjoying the beach or a walk on the island. Bedrooms are 1 king sleep number bed, 1 queen and 1 bedroom with a double and twin bunk bed and a second twin bed. The living room includes queen sleeper sofa, fireplace and flat screen TV. The house includes wireless internet and cable, flat screen TV's throughout, washer and dryer, outdoor shower, and fish cleaning station. This house has a comfortable kitchen with standard appliances including blender, toaster open, coffee maker, and microwave.
