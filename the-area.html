The AREA

We are proud of our quiet little paradise on the north coast of Florida. Much of this part of Florida remains undeveloped and represents historic Florida of yesteryear. Enjoy a visit to our historic St. George Lighthouse. Grab some wine or fresh baked bread from one of our local shops, Sometimes it’s Hotter, Happy Hour is great way to end you day with fresh oysters at Paddy’s or Doc Martin’s.  Pick up fresh off the boat seafood from our local vendors on your way home for a great dinner.

Just a short drive away, the historic city of Apalachicola boasts quaint shops, restaurants and a historic soda fountain complete with old fashioned ice cream floats. Stroll the waterfront, shop for nautical treasures, local sponges, handmade items, designer clothes, or something special for your pampered pet. Enjoy a cold one at our local brewery, Oyster City Brewery.  Eat at Up the Creek or Tamara’s and sample the local fresh seafood or enjoy famous Apalachicola oysters and a beautiful sunset on the deck.
